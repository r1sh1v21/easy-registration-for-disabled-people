Assistance for Disabled Persons

The government scheme for disabled people aims to provide the necessary aid and assistance to individuals who have disabilities, ensuring that they can lead independent lives with dignity and respect. The scheme provides a range of services such as medical assistance, rehabilitation, education, job training, and financial support to help individuals with disabilities access the resources they need to thrive. By supporting disabled people in this way, the government scheme aims to break down barriers and promote inclusivity, helping to create a more equitable society for all.
